Artist's DB

Currently, Dyson is working on a comic called "Vayfe". Vayfe is a story about the struggles of a dragonet who is trying to defeat a powerful enemy known as "The Darq". Vayfe is a three volume body of work. Dyson is currently working on the third volume.

Dyson is also working with a group of comic writers and storytellers to create a comic called "Valuable Comics". Dyson has been involved since volume 9 of the comics.

In 2011 Dyson started a comic called "Shenanigans". It is a 32 page comic book is loosely based on Dyson, his wife and Dyson's three older sisters.

From 2002 to about 2005, Dyson was working in Edmonton helping publishing four magazine (Kidsafe, Community Drug Alert, Kids in Motion and EnviroKids). Kidsafe promoted safety for younger children, Community Drug Alert was intended for preteens and teens to raise awareness about drug and alcohol abuse. Kids in Motion was intended for youths with disabilites. It contained games, articles and activies. Envirokids raised awareness on environmental issues.

Dyson has also done art work for a music album for the band "Symptom Number Seven" and logos for a variety of businesses.
