About

Charley was born in France, the year of 1946 and became a Canadian in 1969. Farrero speaks french, spanish, and of course english.From 1972-1975, he studied at the University of Regina, Extension and Fine Arts Ceramics. Yet beforehand, he took some science classes at "Faculte des Sciences in Paris, France. Now he dwells in Meacham, Saskatchewan, which also inhabits his studio.




Charley Farrero at work in Jalisco, Mexico

This artist is well known for his salt glazes and his use of different kilns. Charley also worked all around the world with all kinds of artists.
