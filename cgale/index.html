Bio



Connie Gale Morgan was originnaly from a small community called Old Wives in SouthWestern Saskatchewan! In 1990 Connie Gale had an interest in a carrer with justice and Received an honors degree in justice. Her job was to deal with "neighbours complaining about each other". In 2001 she has made the choice to pursue an art degree. In 2006 Connie attended a small art studio in Saskatoon (Dru Blair Art Studio), where she developed an intrest in air brushing. She specializes in airbrush art. In April 2006 she completed her photo realism course. She completed her portrait class in august 2006. Connie is currently residing in Northeastern Saskatchewan. Connie practices Airbrushing, Sandblasting, Photography, Painting on Drift-Wood,Dog Tags, and Jewelry!
