Sawblades

Grace did these paintings on circular sawblades. The blades with the hummingbird and schoolhouse have been given a coat of laquer, and the others have not.

This blade was painted from a photo taken around harvest time. Grace gave the blade as a gift

This picture of a hummingbird was painted from another picture. It was also given as a gift.

This painting of an old schoolhouse was painted from a black and white photo.

This picture of a farmyard was painted from a photo.
