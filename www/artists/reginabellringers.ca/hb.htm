Heather Britton

Heather has dedicated her life to the education of young people. She is a world traveler who has explored many exotic places through the world. When Heather was studying in Oxford England she established her taste for tower bell ringing.

Heather has provided much guidance in the apprenticeship program and the building of repertoire. Heather has performed many concerts and has led the apprenticeship program for Carman Price and Candace Wolf. Heather is a congregational leader at Westminster United Church.
