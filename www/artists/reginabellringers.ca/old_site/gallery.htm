Dead Air Space Gallery

 

In between the ringing room and the bells there is a space.  We call it the dead air space.  This is the buffer between the ringers and the bells which probably saves the ear drums of the ringers in the long run.

 

As part of the bell ringers continued effort to contribute to the arts, the Regina Bell Ringers have created a rustic gothic environment to display some of the communities’ hidden works. 

 

The season for the gallery is the same for the tours of the bells.  From May long weekend to September long weekend.
