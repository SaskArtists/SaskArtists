The Art of Change Ringing

Bell ringing demands concentration and co-ordination of body and mind. Ringing a change requires a lot more mental than physical energy.

The ancient art of change ringing is based on the law of mathematics which determines how many changes are possible with any given number of bells. In each peal, the order changes until all possible combinations have been used up. While more than seven bells are seldom used at one time, if all bells in the Darke Memorial Chimes (12) were to be used at one time, this would constitute a "maximus" peal which would permit close to five billion changes and take 137 years to complete.
