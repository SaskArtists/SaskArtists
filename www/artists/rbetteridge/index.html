Robert Betteridge was born in the same year when the Hindenberg fell on May 6, 1937. What led to his career was his destiny. He was born in a little town called Neptune and 50 miles south from Weybern. His first piece of art was named "Golgothan Day Requiem" and "Cyclopean Repeas" (his first art to gain notoriety) He has a gallery on Sunset Boulevard and he has 2 siblings. His most recent piece of art is an audio CD with 40 minutes of him talking. He never made money from his art and it's all non-profit. His hobbies beside art is daydreaming and thinking plus leaving marks and leaving poetry.
