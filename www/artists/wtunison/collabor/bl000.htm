The BIG LIE is often the forcing of someone to change their self for a reward of happiness, contentment, or even privilege. The education system has played a big part in perpetrating the BIG LIE. In the 1950's the practice of standardizing writing with the right hand was abandoned but those who made the switch, were they actually buying into the BIG LIE.

Miss Ramsey and my parents struck a deal for my own good to guide me into the NORMAL world of right handedness. The switch was not devastating at age five but it did leave me clumsy and confused in a bi-directional sense (in other words 'Which is the right way?'). This change in self was to open the doors of acceptance. It was a lie.
