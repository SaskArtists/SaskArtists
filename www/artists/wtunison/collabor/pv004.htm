Prayer for Logical Justice with Mercy

Living God, who shines forth in unbounded darkened existence. It is you who give us hope, it is you who create our solutions, it is you who show us inner peace.

Lord Jesus we need your loving grace to heal our wounded world. The tribal strife of Africa and Europe scorching the flesh, architecture and land needs your power to restore the calm, the quiet, the fertility. From the ashes humble beginnings could glow. When nature has been ravished by human foolishness give the world a new life, breath on it with your healing breath.
