Grace

Almighty Father
Source of divine wisdom
Igniter of true inspiration
Calm our minds
and grant us the tranquility
that we may open our Bibles
and learn from the words
you have set down there for us
May we learn to take the high road
to find our own holiness and let us
find guidance with the
scroll of our faith.
