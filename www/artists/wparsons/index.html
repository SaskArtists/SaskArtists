Wendy Parsons

Wendy Parsons holds a Fine Arts degree from the University of Regina and a Masters degree in Museum Studies from the University of Leicester.U.K. She has been the Resident Artist for both the cities of Weyburn and Moose Jaw. Wendy has worked for the Moose Jaw Museum & Art Gallery as the education coordinator and the Organization of Saskatchewan Arts Councils. Wendy Parsons also has done artistic collaborations with her husband Zach Dietrich.
