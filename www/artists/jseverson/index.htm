ARTIST

Jack is one of the finest artists from Saskatchewan. In his works, colours are vibrant,
dancing around the picture showing motion, happiness, and sadness bringing to life the pen
and ink figures hidden on the paintings. Of late, Jack has been heavily involved with the S.I.F.C.
Indian Arts department at the University of Regina, influencing young artists with his talent
and bike riding skills. (He now owns a VAN!)
