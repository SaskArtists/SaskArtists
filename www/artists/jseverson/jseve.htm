ARTIST

Jack is one of the finest artists from Saskatchewan. In his works, colours are vibrant,
dancing around the picture showing motion, happiness, and saddness bringing to life the pen
and ink figures hidden on the paintings. Jack had been heavily involved with the SIFC
Indian Arts department at the University of Regina, influencing young artists with his talent
and bike riding skills. He worked at the University of Regina until 2003,
he now lives in Regina and continues to create art in his free time.
