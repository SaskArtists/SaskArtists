Marsha's awards and Shows

  • Awards and Shows 1977
  • B.F.A. University of Regina 1981
  • MFA York University 1995
  • Rosemont Art Gallery (Regina) 1997
  • Art Gallery of Swift Current 1999
  • Mackenzie Art Gallery 2001
  • Medicine Hat Museum and Art Gallery 2006
  • Vernon Art Gallery, Vernon, British Columbia
  • Prairie Art Gallery, Grand Prairie, Alberta. Mapping the Land and Body. Solo Exhibition. 2007
  • Vernon Art Gallery, Vernon, British Columbia. Mapping the Land and Body. Solo exhibition 2007
  • Mysteria Art Gallery, Regina, Saskatchewan. The Rapture of Flora. Solo exhibition. 2008
  • Mysteria Art Gallery, Regina, Saskatchewan. Birds, Nests, Water, Sky. Gallery B. 2009
  • Mysteria Art Gallery, Regina, Saskatchewan. Remains. Marsha Kennedy and Tracy Templeton. 2009
  • Godrey Dean Art Gallery, Yorkton, Saskatchewan. Rapture. Marsha Kennedy and Marlo V. 2010
  • Mysteria Gallery, Regina, Saskatchewan. Upon My Lap the World is at Play. Other artist: Carol Epp, A Collection of Tainted Virtue. 2010
  • Kennedy published the limited edition art book The Rapture of Flora, with an essay by Jack Anderson and the poem The Metamorphosis of Plants by Goethe. 2011
  • Kennedy was part of an “Artists by Artists” program through the Mendel Art Gallery (Saskatoon), in which she mentored sculptor Carole Epp
