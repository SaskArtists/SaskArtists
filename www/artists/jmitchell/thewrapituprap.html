The Wrap It Up Rap

My headache it aches
and my fingers won't work
and I'd like to throttle
that miserable jerk
who made this darn bottle
who's top wont come lose
the Tylenol company has no excuse
for treating the pain-laden public this way
come to think of it
wrappers all should be slapped
for wrapping up things
that should never be wrapped
and you have to try scissors
or maybe a hammer
along with the f word
if you'll pardon my grammar
we should throw the whole bunch of them into the slammer!
