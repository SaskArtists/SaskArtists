Saskatchewan Artists

June Mitchell's work has been published in literary magazines such as Grain and Antigonish Review and magazines such as Spring, Briar patch and Transition. She has also been published in newsletters such as; Grey Matters, and Network and anthologies such as Celebrating Poets Over Seventy, and Ya Shoulda Been There 5, Chapbook, and Traces of a Traveller. She has since added And I Think to Myself as well as a memoir titled Glad I Dropped In.
