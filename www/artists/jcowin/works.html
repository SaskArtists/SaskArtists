Jack shared his love for dogs as a hobby and also a business. When out with his hunting friends, Jack always was able to identify the special canines. He utilized his talents and sold many portraits of different dogs.

Jack was a fisherman and expressed his love for them in his drawings. Sometimes he'd catch them with his buddies or his buddies would catch them for him and he would draw. He used special pencil crayons for his fishes and used etchings to make his paintings stand out.
