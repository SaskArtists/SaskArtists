At the Beach

At the Beach is 8 x 11 inches and done in conte on black paper. This series is exploring the human body as a landscape of colours. I drew the secondary colours I saw in the flesh tones. The secondary colours are the ones that you mix with flesh tone to create the shadow and light to create a 3-d look. I also do portraits in conte.
