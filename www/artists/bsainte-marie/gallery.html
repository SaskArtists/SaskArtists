This is a self portrait of Buffy that she had on her computer then played with and added some interesting colours while she was wearing a light veil.

This is an image of a Native elder that is inside a valley, in which teepees are visible. It shows the elders wisdom and how he has experienced chaos and destruction.

This image is based on a photo made by renown British photographer Simon Fowler. It was originally used for the cover of her CD Coincidence and Likely Stories. Buffy worked with the image in Photoshop duplicating, then reversing the image, and intertwining the arms. She then painted it, adding cosmic spheres and other details.
