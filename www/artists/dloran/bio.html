David Loran was born on March 11, 1992 in Regina Saskatchewan. He began drawing at a young age.He went to school at Miller High School and went to special art classes that were at Leboldus High School.
He currently works at Colours Art and Framing which is a paint shop in Regina. He enjoys sketching in his notebook and has many notebooks full of sketches and drawings. He enjoys listening to music, some of his favourite artists are Def 3, Rubiks, Taylor Swift, and Common. In his free time you will usually find him either doing some sort of art, playing video games, or just hanging out with his friends.
If you asked him what kind of style he has when he does his art he would tell you that "I have a juxtaposition of realism and portrait style with graffiti style". He is apart of a art crew that is called the 11 hooks crew, which is some artists that get together to make awesome murals. They get asked by lots of people to do art murals. One of his favourtie quotes is "Playing it safe is the most popular way to fail". You can see more of his work and even contact him, he is on facebook as David loran. He is aviable to do murals and other art if you contact him.
