Zane Wilcox

Zane Wilcox is a ceramic artist establish in Saskatoon. He works in a wide assortment of formats, from operational pottery to purely sculptural work. Zane makes beautiful vases, little cups, and a variety of many other things. His art is polished and smothered in deep rich colors, pastels and palettes of many other tones.
