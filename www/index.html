About SaskArtists

SaskArtists is a collection of artists born, raised, or primarily risiding in Saskatchewan. The website is maintained and updated by attendees of the MSI Computer Camps hosted anually at the University of Regina.

Our Goal

Our goal is to provide a resource for learning about and discovering Saskatchewan Artists while educating the next generation of online creators. Websites are created, updated, fixed, mantained and improved by students between the ages of 14 and 18. All pages are ran through code quality assesments, error detection, and spelling checks before becoming visible on this site. We periodically check the contents of pages to make sure the content stays up-to-date.
