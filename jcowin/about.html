About Him

Timeline

  • Born in July of 1947
  • Received Bachelor of Fine Arts in 1969 from University of Indiana
  • Received master of Fine Arts in 1971 from University of Illinois
  • Became a professor and started teaching Printmaking at the University of Regina in 1971
  • Married to Mary and had two children
  • Retired from his teaching career in 2007
  • Died in September of 2014

Talents:

  • Fisherman
  • Printmaker
  • Artist
