Save Us From the X-Files

Lord of Hosts, giver of law, light and life, lead us to understand those mysteries we need to assist in the execution of your will. When we read of flaming chariots do not let us see saucers from Mars unless it is your will. When we read of encounters with heroic culture figures let us put the X-files out of our minds unless it is your will. Cool our millennium fever. May we bath in all dimensions of the true light which you let shine so brightly in the scroll of our faith. Let it guide our actions and lead us toward your sanctified ways.

Amen
